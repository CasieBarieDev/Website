Zuipse!

In Development


'Zuipse!' is an upcoming mobile game that aims to bring the beloved drinking game 'Ride the Bus' to your mobile device. Currently in development, 'Zuipse!' promises to deliver an immersive and entertaining experience for players looking to enjoy this classic game with friends in a digital format.


Follow my development progress as we work to bring 'Zuipse!' to life. Join our community and be among the first to experience this exciting mobile adaptation of 'Ride the Bus'!
