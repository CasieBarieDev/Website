LightTiles

LightTiles is an intuitive Android app that enhances your quick settings by adding custom tiles to control your Philips Hue lights directly from the notification shade.


With LightTiles, you can easily turn your Hue lights on or off without needing to open any apps, making smart home lighting control faster and more convenient.

Features

  • Quick Settings Integration
    • Add custom tiles to your Android quick settings panel to instantly control your Philips Hue lights.
  • Instant On/Off Control
    • Toggle your Hue lights on or off directly from the notification shade.
  • Brightness Control
    • Access a mini menu to adjust the brightness of your lights without opening any additional apps.
  • Color Reset
    • Easily reset your lights to their default color with a single tap.
  • No Additional App Needed
    • Simplify your smart home experience by avoiding any app launches.

Please note that this app is not publicly available. Since Android has introduced more efficient ways to control your home, I do not plan to release it in the future.
