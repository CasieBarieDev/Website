Hear the Painting

For my study, I explored the idea of describing a painting using senses other than sight, focusing specifically on sound.


I envisioned a scenario where blind individuals visiting a museum could touch a painting while hearing sounds that bring the artwork to life. To illustrate this concept, I created a small webpage as a proof of concept.


Note: The webpage is in Dutch and is not optimized for small screens.
