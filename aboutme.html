Personal Life

Ever since I was young, I’ve been drawn to technology and creativity, always looking for ways to bring ideas to life. That passion led me to a job as an all-round technician at business events, DJ shows, and small festivals. There’s something exciting about being behind the scenes, making sure everything runs smoothly, whether it’s the perfect lighting for a DJ set or the right setup for a big event. It’s a mix of problem-solving, precision, and adrenaline that never gets old.


But life isn’t just about work and coding. Some of my favorite moments are the simple ones, hanging out with friends, going on spontaneous adventures, or just relaxing together. I also love video editing, especially turning those fun moments into something we can look back on. There's something satisfying about capturing memories and making them even more special.


Whenever I need a break, I take my dog, Billy, for a walk. It’s the perfect way to clear my mind, get some fresh air, and just enjoy the moment. Swimming is another hobby that helps me unwind. It keeps me active and gives me space to think, making sure I stay energized for my next project.


And through it all, I’m lucky to have an amazing girlfriend who supports me and shares so many of my interests. Whether it’s work, hobbies, or just everyday life, having someone by my side makes everything even better.
