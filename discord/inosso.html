InOsso

InOsso is a feature-rich Discord music bot designed to bring high-quality music playback and a variety of music-related functionalities to your Discord server.


Created for friends, InOsso aims to enhance your server's entertainment experience with seamless music streaming, playlist management, and user-friendly commands. Designed with simplicity in mind, InOsso ensures that users of all experience levels can enjoy its features.


Since this bot is designed for friends who speak Dutch, all the images shown on this page will be in Dutch.

Features

  • Music playing:
    • Send a song name, and it will display the top 5 results from YouTube for you to choose from.
    • It has music control buttons such as pausing and skipping.
    • All songs are added to a queue you can view with the /queue command.
    • If you send a playlist link, all the songs will be added to the queue.
  • /clearchat command.
  • Deletes non video messages from the 'Clips' channel.
  • A command to choose a random person from the voicecall. (/kies)
  • A command to ping everyone to join the game. Each person can accept or decline the request, informing others of their decision. (/komen)
  • A command to grant a visitor access to the channels to play with us. (/gast)
  • Some fun easter egg commands.
