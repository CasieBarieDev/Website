InOsso

InOsso is a feature-rich Discord music bot designed to bring high-quality music playback and a variety of music-related functionalities to your Discord server.


Created for friends, InOsso aims to enhance your server's entertainment experience with seamless music streaming, playlist management, and user-friendly commands. Designed with simplicity in mind, InOsso ensures that users of all experience levels can enjoy its features.


Since this bot is designed for friends who speak Dutch, all the images shown on this page will be in Dutch.

Features

  • Music playing:
    • Send a song name, and it will display the top 5 results from SoundCloud for you to choose from.
    • Control playback with buttons for pausing, skipping, and replaying.
    • All songs go into a queue, which you can view by clicking the 'Wachtrij' button.
    • The queue can be cleared, and the last added song can be moved to the top.
    • Sending a playlist link adds all its songs to the queue.
  • Automatically deletes non-video messages from the 'Clips' channel.
  • /kies - Selects a random person from the voice call.
  • /komen - Pings everyone to join the game. People can accept or decline, informing others of their decision.
  • /gast - Grants or removes a visitor's access to join the voicecall.
  • /stuur - Sends a message as InOsso.
  • /help - Provides information on available features.
